About Me

I'm quite boring, really.

focused on the finer details

I'm Mia, a developer who got started with Java at age 10 by making video game mods. That early curiosity grew into a life-long passion and laid the foundation for a career in software and systems development.

These days, I spend most of my time on web development and creating internal tooling, though I regularly explore new technologies through hobby projects. Recently, this has included a deep dive into local AI hosting and RAG systems.
I enjoy refining development workflows and building things that make life easier for myself and the people I work with. I'm passionate about writing maintainable code, building automation, and designing infrastructure that’s painless to work with.
I chase that feeling of planning out a clean software architecture and seeing it come alive through development.

I love learning and expanding my grasp of contemporary technologies. Collaboration plays a massive role in helping me achieve this, as it provides access to mindsets completely dissimilar to mine, which often reveal details I overlooked or missed
Working closely alongside peers has taught me a lot, especially by seeing how they approach problem-solving and use tools or techniques that are unfamiliar to me. These fresh perspectives have often inspired me to rethink and improve my workflows.
I love working alongside my friends <3
